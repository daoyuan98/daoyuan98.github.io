Short Bio

I obtained my PhD degree from National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Mohan Kankanhalli in 2024. My research interests include Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLM) and Human-Object Interaction Detection. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree of Computer Science from Zhejiang University in June, 2019. During my study, I had the pleasure to intern at Microsoft Research (Redmond), Tencent ARC Lab, Didi Chuxing and Hikvision Research Institute.
I am always open to collaborations! Feel free to contact me if you find a joint research interets with me.
