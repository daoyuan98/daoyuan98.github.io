Biography

I'm currently a 3rd year (2020-) PhD student at National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Mohan Kankanhalli. I am affiliated at both school of computing (SoC) and institute of data science (IDS). Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree of Computer Science from Zhejiang University in June, 2019. My research interests include human-centric scene understanding, vision and language and knowledge transfer.
